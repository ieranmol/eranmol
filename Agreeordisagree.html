Writing Task 2 || 7+ Band Format

Agree or disagree

Don’t discuss views of other people in this essay.

Intro
1. Paraphrasing
2. (100% One sided opinion)
However, I do not agree that we should therefore force all teenagers to do unpaid work. (30-70 answer)
While I agree that some traditional ideas are outdated, I believe that others are still useful and should not be forgotten.

Body Paragraph 1
1. Justify first mentioned question in intro.
2. In my opinion, …. ; (for 100%)Firstly, I think that….,
Or start generally
3. 1st point (Strong Point) followed by an explanation and example. 2nd point followed by an explanation and example(optional).

Body Paragraph 2
1. Justify second mentioned question in intro.
2. However, …. ; (for 100%)At the same time,…
3. 1st point (Strong Point) followed by an explanation and example. 2nd point followed by an explanation and example(optional).

Conclusion
1. In conclusion, teenagers may choose to work for free and help others, in my opinion we should not make this compulsory.
2. In conclusion, although the views of older people may sometimes seem unhelpful in todays world, we should not dismiss all traditional ideas as irrelevant.
