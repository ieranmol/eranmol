Writing Task 2 || 7+ Band Format

Positive or negative development

Whole body is your opinion.
Discuss both positives and negatives.

Intro
1. Paraphrase
2. It is true that, __________. Although there are some drawbacks of______(topic)___, I would argue that there are far more benefits.
3. In my opinion, this trend could have both positive and negative consequences in equal measures.

Body Paragraph 1
1. Justify first mentioned question in intro.
2. In my opinion/view, ….
Or start generally
The main drawbacks of the trend towards_______....
3. 1st point (Strong Point) followed by an explanation and example. 2nd point followed by an explanation and example(optional).

Body Paragraph 2
1. Justify second mentioned question in intro.
2. However, ….
Or generally
Despite the negatives mentioned above, I believe that….
3. 1st point (Strong Point) followed by an explanation and example. 2nd point followed by an explanation and example(optional).

Conclusion
1. in conclusion, while I recognize the possible adverse outcomes of_____(topic)______, I consider it to be a positive development.
2. In conclusion, the increase in ____(topic)____ will have both beneficial and detrimental effects on individual and on the economy.
